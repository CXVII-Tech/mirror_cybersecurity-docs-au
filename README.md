# A mirror of [`cybersecurity-docs-au`](https://github.com/CXVII-Tech/cybersecurity-docs-au)

This repository provides a manually maintained, Markdown-formatted mirror of publicly available Australian cybersecurity documentation.

All content is manually copied and reformatted, ensuring full human oversight over structure, scope, and changes.

## Purpose

To provide a machine-readable, Markdown-based mirror of public Australian cybersecurity information for educational and research use.

## Scope

- Includes publicly accessible pages from Australian government websites that contain cybersecurity advice, publications, and frameworks.
- Content is stored in Markdown (.md) format.
- No proprietary or restricted materials are included.
- Updates are performed manually by a human on an ad-hoc basis.

 ### Out of Scope

- Automated scraping or data harvesting
- Non-public or sensitive information
- Any modification to the substance of the government's original guidance
