This application form has been optimised to work on desktop devices. If you experience any issues completing this form on a mobile or tablet device, please try using a desktop device instead.

## **Application requirements**

In order to submit your application you acknowledge that you have met the following requirements:

- I have read and understand the [IRAP Policy and Procedures](https://www.cyber.gov.au/sites/default/files/2023-02/IRAP%20Policy%20and%20Procedures.pdf)
- I have 5 years of technical ICT experience with at least 2 years of information security experience on systems using the Information Security Manual and supporting publications.
- I have completed the IRAP Training course and passed the examination.

Are you an Australian Citizen? Yes/No
