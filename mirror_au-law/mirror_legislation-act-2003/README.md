# A mirror of [Legislation Act 2003](https://github.com/CXVII-Tech/cybersecurity-docs-au/tree/main/au-law/legislation-act-2003)

The [Legislation Act 2003](legislation-act-2003.md), formerly the Legislative Instruments Act 2003 (LIA), provides the framework for the management of Commonwealth legislation, including the making, registration, publication, parliamentary scrutiny and sunsetting (automatic repeal) of Commonwealth delegated legislation.
